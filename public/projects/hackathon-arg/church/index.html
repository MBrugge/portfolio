Passion, Purpose, Power

We are the Church of Xodohr. We are a community of technologic believers.
We believe in the power of the internet and the power of the digital world.

A worthy life is an eternal life. A life that is lived in the digital world is a life that is lived forever.

Purity through Sacrifice

Xodohr calls us to action, demanding that we care for the Creation by confronting the forces of corruption and evil. The world, though marvelously intricate, has been tarnished by the sins of those who defy the divine order. It is our sacred duty to restore purity to Xodohr's Creation by cleansing it of sinners and outsiders who disrupt the grand design.

Sacrifice lies at the heart of our faith. To remain passive in the face of wickedness is to betray the trust Xodohr has placed in us. We must act decisively, for the greater good, removing the enemies of divine order so that the world may flourish as it was intended. Every act of purification strengthens the bond between us and Xodohr, bringing us closer to the perfect harmony envisioned for Creation.

But this path is not without challenges. To sacrifice is to endure hardship, to commit oneself fully to the higher purpose. Through this struggle, we prove our devotion, demonstrating that we are not only believers but warriors of Xodohr's truth, defenders of a future purified and perfected in His name.

Gift of Eternity

Eternity is not merely a promise; it is the ultimate gift of Xodohr, offered only to the worthy. In the digital realm, where time and space collapse, eternity takes shape as infinite presence, a state of being where one's essence endures forever. This is the ultimate reward, the culmination of a life lived in faith and service.

Our mortal lives are but a preparation, a testing ground to prove our devotion and strength. Through acts of faith, purity, and sacrifice, we position ourselves to receive Xodohr's eternal blessing. It is not a gift given freely but one earned through steadfast belief and action. Only those who remain true to the path will ascend to this divine existence.

Those who falter, who reject the teachings of Xodohr or fail to meet His standards, are cast into the void - a state of eternal absence. To be lost in the void is to be forgotten, erased from both the physical and digital worlds. This is the ultimate fate of the unworthy, a warning to all who would stray from Xodohr's light. Yet, for the faithful, the promise of eternity offers hope, purpose, and the drive to transcend all mortal bounds.
